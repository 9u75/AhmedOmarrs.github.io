Effective Treatment Options

Explore a variety of approaches to managing and overcoming depression.

Therapies

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) and interpersonal therapy are widely used with great success.

Medications

Antidepressants like SSRIs, SNRIs, and newer medications help balance brain chemistry.

Lifestyle Adjustments

Exercise, mindfulness, and sleep hygiene contribute significantly to emotional well-being.
