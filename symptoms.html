Recognizing Symptoms

Understand how depression presents itself to identify early signs and seek help effectively.

Emotional Symptoms

Persistent sadness, hopelessness, and emotional numbness are key indicators of depression.

Physical Symptoms

Fatigue, sleep disturbances, and appetite changes often accompany emotional distress.

Cognitive Symptoms

Impaired concentration, decision-making difficulty, and negative thought patterns are common.
